Safety First

Biohacking should make you feel better, not worse. If symptoms increase, we simplify and reset.

Safety rules​

  • No aggressive fasting during heavy training weeks.
  • Prioritize sleep before increasing intensity.
  • Use supplements only with a clear goal and exit plan.
  • Consult your clinician for medical conditions or medications.
